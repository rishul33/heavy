European Capitals Quiz

Question: Slovenia
Answer: Ljubljana is the capital of Slovenia.
Question: Lithuania
Answer: Vilnius is the capital of Lithuania.
Question: Portugal
Answer: Lisbon is the capital of Portugal.
Question: Romania
Answer: Bucharest is the capital of Romania.
Question: Estonia
Answer: Tallinn is the capital of Estonia.
Question: Russia
Answer: Moscow is the capital of Russia.
Question: Poland
Answer: Warsaw is the capital of Poland.
Question: Ukraine
Answer: Kyiv is the capital of Ukraine.
Question: Sweden
Answer: Stockholm is the capital of Sweden.
Question: Switzerland
Answer: Bern is the capital of Switzerland.
Question: Serbia
Answer: Belgrade is the capital of Serbia.
Question: Netherlands
Answer: Amsterdam is the capital of the Netherlands. The Hague is its seat of government.
Question: Slovakia
Answer: Bratislava is the capital of Slovakia.
Question: Iceland
Answer: Reykjavík is the capital of Iceland.
Question: Croatia
Answer: Zagreb is the capital of Croatia.
Question: Spain
Answer: Madrid is the capital of Spain.
Question: Monaco
Answer: Strictly speaking, the Principality of Monaco is a single administrative unit, and no separate area within it is distinguished as capital, but it is conventional to identify “Monaco” as its own capital.
Question: Bosnia and Herzegovina
Answer: Sarajevo is the capital of Bosnia and Herzegovina.
Question: North Macedonia
Answer: Skopje is the capital of North Macedonia.
Question: Finland
Answer: Helsinki is the capital of Finland.
Question: Ireland
Answer: Dublin is the capital of Ireland.
Question: Luxembourg
Answer: Luxembourg (the city) is the capital of Luxembourg (the country).
Question: France
Answer: Paris is the capital of France.
Question: Georgia
Answer: Tbilisi is the capital of Georgia.
Question: Hungary
Answer: Budapest is the capital of Hungary.
Question: Belgium
Answer: Brussels is the capital of Belgium.
Question: Kosovo
Answer: Pristina is the capital of Kosovo, which declared itself independent in 2008.
Question: Bulgaria
Answer: Sofia is the capital of Bulgaria.
Question: San Marino
Answer: San Marino (the city) is the capital of San Marino (the republic).
Question: Andorra
Answer: Andorra la Vella is the capital of Andorra.
Question: Turkey
Answer: Ankara is the capital of Turkey.
Question: Germany
Answer: Berlin is the capital of Germany.
Question: Albania
Answer: Tirana is the capital of Albania.
Question: Greece
Answer: Athens is the capital of Greece.
Question: Belarus
Answer: Minsk is the capital of Belarus.
Question: Austria
Answer: Vienna is the capital of Austria.
Question: Italy
Answer: Rome is the capital of Italy.
Question: Moldova
Answer: Chișinău is the capital of Moldova.
Question: Latvia
Answer: Riga is the capital of Latvia.
Question: Montenegro
Answer: Podgorica is the capital of Montenegro. (Cetinje was the historical capital.)
Question: United Kingdom
Answer: London is the capital of the United Kingdom.
Question: Norway
Answer: Oslo is the capital of Norway.
Question: Armenia
Answer: Yerevan is the capital of Armenia.
Question: Denmark
Answer: Copenhagen is the capital of Denmark.
Question: Malta
Answer: Valletta is the capital of Malta.
Question: Liechtenstein
Answer: Vaduz is the capital of Liechtenstein.
Question: Czech Republic
Answer: Prague is the capital of the Czech Republic (Czechia).
